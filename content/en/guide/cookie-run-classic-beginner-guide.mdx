<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics and Controls">

Success in CookieRun Classic is determined by your proficiency with the two-button interface. While the controls are simple to learn, the level design introduces increasingly complex patterns of Jellies and obstacles that require precise execution. Every run is a balance between "greed" (reaching for risky Jellies) and "survival" (avoiding hits that deplete your health bar).

The scoring system is multifaceted. You earn points by collecting basic Jellies, special power-ups, and through the unique skills of your chosen Cookie and Pet. Obstacles are your primary enemy; hitting one not only reduces your health but also breaks your momentum, which can be devastating in competitive League runs.

| Control Type | Action | Scoring Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Jump** | Tap Jump once to clear small gaps | Maintains momentum and collects mid-air Jellies |
| **Double Jump** | Tap Jump twice to reach high platforms | Essential for reaching secret paths and Bonus Time |
| **Slide** | Hold Slide to pass under low obstacles | Necessary for survival in narrow corridors |
| **Skill Trigger** | Automatic based on character cooldown | Provides massive score boosts or invincibility |
