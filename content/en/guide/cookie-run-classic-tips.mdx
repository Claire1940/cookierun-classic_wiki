<Section icon={Target} title="Core Mechanics: Mastering Jump and Slide">

The heart of **cookie run classic tips** lies in mastering the simplicity of the two-button control scheme. While it may seem easy to tap your way through the early stages, the difficulty spikes significantly as you enter higher League tiers. Precision movement is the difference between a record-breaking score and a premature end to your run.

**Video Highlights:**
- Mastery of the double jump for clearing large gaps.
- Timing slides to maximize Jelly collection in narrow corridors.
- Identifying obstacle hitboxes to avoid unnecessary damage.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Heroic Mistakes">
Many players lose momentum by attempting to collect every single Jelly on the screen. It is better to skip a risky Jelly than to take an obstacle hit that breaks your score multiplier or ends the run entirely.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Muscle Memory">
    Practice the timing of the double jump. Many obstacles in the later stages of a run require a specific delay between the first and second tap to clear safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Slide">
    Sliding is not just for low ceilings. It can also be used to adjust your horizontal positioning slightly, helping you line up for the next set of Jellies or jumps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn the Route">
    Each stage has a fixed layout. Pay attention to the patterns of Jellies and obstacles. Knowing what is coming next allows you to prepare your jumps in advance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Using your **Supreme Treasure Tickets** wisely is one of the most important **cookie run classic tips**. You should aim for Treasures that provide "All Jelly" score increases or magnetic effects that draw Jellies toward you, reducing the need for risky movement.

Nothing ruins a high-score streak like a technical issue or lost data. Because CookieRun Classic is a live-service game, your progress is stored on Devsisters' servers, but only if your account is properly linked.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Link to DevPlay">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Social Links">
    As a backup, link your Apple ID or Google Play account. This provides a secondary recovery method if you lose access to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Storage">

  </Step>
