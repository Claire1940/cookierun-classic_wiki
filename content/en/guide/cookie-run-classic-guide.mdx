<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Running Mechanics & Controls">

The core loop of **CookieRun Classic** is designed around accessibility and precision. With only two buttons—**Jump** and **Slide**—success depends on your ability to read the terrain and react to the speed of the run. As you progress, the speed increases, requiring faster reflexes to collect Jellies while avoiding obstacles that deplete your Cookie's health.

New players should focus on the **"Witch's Oven"** escape routes. These early stages are designed to teach you the rhythm of the game. Pay close attention to the placement of Giant Jellies and Blast Jelly power-ups, as these provide temporary invincibility and massive score boosts.

| Jelly Type | Effect | Scoring Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Jelly** | Small score increase | Low |
| **Alphabet Jelly** | Collect for "BONUSTIME" | High |
| **Blast Jelly** | High speed + Invincibility | Very High |
| **Giant Jelly** | Cookie grows + Destroys obstacles | High |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Control Tip">
In the **League** mode, try to "slide-cancel" your jumps. By sliding immediately after landing a jump, you can sometimes avoid obstacles that appear directly after a platform edge.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Account Transfer & Troubleshooting">

Protecting your progress in **CookieRun Classic** is essential, especially with the frequent updates planned for 2026. Because the game is a live service, your save data is stored on Devsisters' servers, but it must be linked to a persistent ID to be accessible across multiple devices.

| Issue Category | Requirement | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Storage** | 3GB - 4GB Free Space | Delete unused apps or clear cache |
| **OS Version** | iOS 15+ / Android 8.0+ | Update system software in settings |
| **Connection** | Stable Wi-Fi/LTE | Avoid public VPNs during League runs |
| **Account Loss** | Linked DevPlay/Apple/Google | Contact support with Player ID |

If you are moving to a new device, simply install the game and log in with the same account provider (Apple, Google, or DevPlay) used on your old device. Your progress, including Cookies, Pets, and purchased Crystals, will sync automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Verify Linking">
    In your old device, go to **Settings** and ensure a social account is listed as "Linked."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare New Device">
    Install CookieRun Classic from the official App Store or Google Play Store.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sync Data">
    On the title screen, select "Sign in" and use the same credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Minimum Requirements">
While the game can be installed on older devices, **League performance** may suffer from frame drops. For competitive play, ensure your device has at least 4GB of RAM and a stable 5GHz Wi-Fi connection.
</Callout>
