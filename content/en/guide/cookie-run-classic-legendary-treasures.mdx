Acquiring and optimizing your **cookie run classic legendary treasures** is a marathon, not a sprint. New players often make the mistake of spreading their resources too thin. Follow this structured approach to ensure you are always running with the most efficient setup possible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Account Leveling">
    Focus on reaching Escape Level 25 as quickly as possible. This is the hard requirement to unlock your third treasure slot. Without three slots, you cannot stack enough multipliers to make high-end farming viable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Hoarding">
    Save the 300 crystals provided in your initial mailbox. Do not spend them on cosmetic items. Use them to pull for the "Potato Salad Cookie" or high-tier treasure chests during rate-up events.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Treasure Selection">
    Identify your goal. If you need coins, equip treasures with "Extra Coins %". If you are pushing for high scores in Land Water, equip treasures that provide "All Jelly Points" or "Energy Drain Reduction".
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enhancement Phase">
    Use Enhancement Stones and Tickets to level up your core treasures. Focus on one treasure at a time until it reaches at least Level 6 before moving to the next.
  </Step>
</Steps>
