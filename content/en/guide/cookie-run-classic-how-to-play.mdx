<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Core Gameplay Mechanics">

The fundamental mechanics of **CookieRun Classic how to play** revolve around two buttons: **Jump** and **Slide**. While the controls are simple, the level design introduces complex patterns of Jellies and obstacles that require precise timing to navigate. Your primary goal is to run as far as possible before your Energy bar depletes, all while accumulating the highest score possible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Jump Mechanic">
    Tap the Jump button on the left side of the screen to leap over gaps and obstacles. Tapping twice performs a Double Jump, allowing you to reach higher platforms or clear larger clusters of hazards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Slide Mechanic">
    Hold the Slide button on the right side of the screen to duck under low-hanging obstacles. Sliding is also essential for collecting Jellies placed in narrow passages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Jelly Collection">
    Jellies are your primary source of points. Different Jellies provide varying score values. Special items like Blast Mode or Giant Mode can help you clear paths and collect Jellies more efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Energy Management">
    Your Cookie's Energy constantly drains over time. Colliding with obstacles causes a significant loss of Energy. Collect Health Potions scattered throughout the map to extend your run.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Obstacle Awareness">
Hitting an obstacle doesn't just reduce Energy; it breaks your momentum. In competitive League runs, a single hit can be the difference between a Gold and Platinum ranking.
</Callout>

| Control Type | Primary Use | Advanced Technique |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Jump** | Clear small gaps / low hurdles | Timing for mid-air Jelly rows |
| **Double Jump** | High platforms / wide pits | Stall in air to avoid projectiles |
| **Slide** | Under low walls / narrow gaps | Quick release to transition into Jump |
| **Blast Mode** | Invincible high-speed dash | Auto-collects all Jellies in path |

Once you have mastered the basics of **CookieRun Classic how to play**, the real challenge lies in the League mode. This is a real-time competitive environment where you are ranked against other players' scores on specific maps. To reach the top of the leaderboard, you need more than just fast fingers; you need a optimized route.

**High Score Strategy Pillars:**
1.  **Route Memorization**: Learn where the largest clusters of Jellies are located and which paths offer the most points.
2.  **Obstacle Avoidance**: Maintaining a "No-Hit" run is crucial. Every hit reduces your Energy and prevents you from reaching the end-of-stage bonuses.
3.  **Active Ability Timing**: Use your Cookie's abilities during "Jelly-dense" areas to maximize the score multiplier.
4.  **Treasure Synergy**: Use Treasures that complement the map. For example, use magnetic Treasures on maps with scattered Jellies.

Protecting your progress is vital in CookieRun Classic. Since the game involves significant time investment and potential in-app purchases, losing access to your account can be devastating. Devsisters provides several ways to secure your data through the DevPlay system.

**Common Troubleshooting Steps:**
- **Game Crashes**: Ensure you have at least 3GB of free space. Close background apps to free up RAM.
- **Connection Issues**: Switch from mobile data to a stable Wi-Fi connection if you experience lag in League races.
- **Update Errors**: Check the App Store or Google Play for the latest version. If the update doesn't appear, try clearing your store cache.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create a DevPlay Account">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enable Cloud Saving">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Note Your Player ID">
    Keep a screenshot of your Player ID (found in Settings). This is required if you ever need to contact support for account recovery.
  </Step>
</Steps>
