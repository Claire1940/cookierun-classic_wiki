To successfully automate **Cookie Run Classic Special Episode 2**, your treasures must handle two things: pulling items from a distance and saving you from the numerous pits found in the Dragon's Valley terrain.   </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pit Lift Setup">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Booster Selection">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Tips for Special Episode 2">

For veteran players, optimizing **Cookie Run Classic Special Episode 2** involves fine-tuning the speed-to-health ratio. If you move too fast, you might miss a box; if you move too slow, you run out of energy.

- **Manual Intervention**: In the later stages of Episode 2, the box positions can be tricky. If you are playing manually, stay in the middle lane to react to boxes spawning on high platforms.
- **Pet Alternatives**: If you don't have Kiwi Bird, the "Flashlight" pet is a decent alternative as it creates bridges over holes, saving your Pit Lift treasures for emergencies.
- **Event Synergy**: During "Global Launch" or collaboration events, Mystery Box drop rates are often doubled. This is the best time to use your saved boosters.
