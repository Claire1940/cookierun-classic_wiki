<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Bonus Time Mechanics">

Bonus Time is a high-reward session that transports your Cookie away from the main track into a specialized world. During this period, your energy does not drain, allowing you to fly freely while collecting massive amounts of jellies and coins. Understanding the rotation of these sessions is key to mastering your run.

The game cycles through different Bonus Time types based on how many times you have triggered the session during a single run. Mastering the rhythm of these transitions allows you to plan your character and pet loadouts more effectively.

| Bonus Time Type | Appearance Frequency | Key Features |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Classic | 1st, 3rd, 5th, etc. | Yellow/Pink bears, Coins, Blast Jellies |
| Space | 2nd, 4th, 6th, etc. | High speed, straight lines, difficult navigation |
| Rainbow Rush | Rare (Low Health) | Massive points, giant gold coins, giant bears |

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Trigger Bonus Time">

There are two primary methods to enter the Bonus Time world. Players who understand these triggers can significantly influence their scoring potential throughout the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Alphabet Letters">
    Gather all letters spelling B-O-N-U-S-T-I-M-E scattered across the stage. Each letter grants 230 points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Bonus Time Jellies">
    Look for specific Bonus Time Jellies in set locations, such as Stage 10 in Escape from the Oven or Stage 6 in Dragon's Valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Pet Abilities">
    Equip specific pets like Cozy Yarn or Magic Pod that can generate Bonus Time Jellies to trigger sessions more frequently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Note">
Be careful when collecting letters. If you pick up a Bonus Time Jelly before completing the full "BONUSTIME" set, your current progress on the letters will be reset. Always finish your sequence if you are close to completion.
</Callout>
