<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Running Mechanics">

The core of **cookie run classic pc** is the two-button system: **Jump and Slide**. While it sounds simple, the level design in the Witch's Oven becomes progressively more complex, requiring frame-perfect inputs to maximize Jelly collection.

- **Double Jumps**: Tap the jump button twice to reach higher platforms or clear large gaps.
- **Extended Slides**: Hold the slide button to pass under low-hanging obstacles. On PC, holding the mapped key is often more precise than touch gestures.
- **Jelly Patterns**: Jellies often form "paths" that guide you toward the safest route. Follow the trail to avoid hidden traps.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Account Transfer & Troubleshooting">

Maintaining your account across different devices is a common concern for **cookie run classic pc** players. Because the game uses DevPlay, you can switch from your Android phone to your PC emulator without losing a single Crystal.

| Issue | Potential Fix |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Game Crashes** | Ensure 3GB+ free storage and restart device |
| **Connection Error** | Switch from Wi-Fi to Data or use a stable LAN on PC |
| **Update Failed** | Clear App Cache or check for OS updates (iOS 15+/AOS 8+) |
| **Missing Rewards** | Restart the app to refresh the Mailbox data |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
**Never delete the app** before linking your account to Google, Apple ID, or DevPlay. Guest accounts are stored locally; if you uninstall the game as a guest, your progress is permanently deleted.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Linking on Mobile">
    Open Settings > Game Info > Link Account. Choose DevPlay and enter a valid email.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Transferring to PC">
    Install the game on PC. On the title screen, select "Login" instead of "Guest" and use your DevPlay credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verification">
    Confirm your Level and Crystal count match before making any in-game purchases on the new platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>
