<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering the Run">

Starting your journey in CookieRun Classic is straightforward, but mastering the leaderboards requires precision. The game focuses on a two-button control scheme that emphasizes timing over complex inputs. Your primary objective is to escape the Witch's Oven while maximizing your Jelly collection.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Basic Control Loop">
    Use the **Jump** button on the left to clear gaps and obstacles. Use the **Slide** button on the right to duck under low-hanging hazards. Double-tapping Jump allows for a higher leap, which is essential for reaching elevated Jelly paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Jelly Prioritization">
    Not all Jellies are created equal. Focus on **Giant Jellies** and **Alphabet Jellies** (which spell out BONUS TIME). Entering Bonus Time is the most effective way to rack up points without the risk of hitting obstacles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing Energy">
    Your Cookie has a depleting energy bar. Collecting **Health Potions** scattered throughout the stage is vital for extending your run. Once your energy hits zero, the run ends unless you have a "Revive" Treasure or Pet ability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Movement Tip">
Avoid "panic jumping." Many obstacles in the later stages are designed to bait a jump, only to hit you with a second hazard in mid-air. Learn the rhythm of the stage before attempting high-score runs.
</Callout>

| Control Type | Action | Best Used For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Jump** | Tap Jump once | Clearing small gaps or single spikes |
| **Double Jump** | Tap Jump twice | Reaching high platforms or clearing walls |
| **Slide** | Hold Slide | Navigating tunnels or low ceilings |
| **Combo Jump** | Jump then Slide | Specialized movement for certain Jelly patterns |
