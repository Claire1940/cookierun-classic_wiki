<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Leveling Up Your Squad">

Maximizing your passive bonuses requires a strategic approach to leveling up your cookies and pets. Because a Cheer Squad's level is determined by the lowest-level member in that squad, leveling up a single legendary cookie to maximum level while leaving others at Level 1 will keep your Cheer Squad stuck at Level 1.

Follow this step-by-step progression guide to efficiently upgrade your legendary squads without wasting precious resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock All Squad Members">
    Acquire at least one copy of every cookie or pet required for your target Cheer Squad. For "The Beginning of A Legend," you must unlock Moonlight, Wind Archer, Sea Fairy, Fire Spirit, and Dark Enchantress. Once all are unlocked at Level 1, the squad's passive bonus automatically activates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Distribute Upgrades Evenly">
    Do not focus all your legendary potions or crystals on a single cookie. Upgrade all members of the squad incrementally. To unlock Level 2 of the Cheer Squad, every single member must be at least Level 2. Keep their levels balanced to ensure steady squad progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor the Menu Indicators">
    Check your Cookie inventory menu regularly. When all members of a Cheer Squad have reached a new level milestone, an arrow icon will appear. Tap this icon to upgrade the Cheer Squad. This upgrade is completely free and does not require coins or crystals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prioritize Legendary Pet Squads">
    Once your legendary cookies are balanced, shift your focus to "The Legendary Meeting" pet squad. Because pets are often easier to pull from specific gachas, you can quickly boost your Giant Star Jelly scores to complement your cookie runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Resource Management">
Always save your Legendary Potions for the lowest-level member of your active squads. This ensures that you can unlock the next Cheer Squad level as quickly as possible, giving you an immediate passive score bump.
</Callout>

</Section>
