Understanding the core loop of the **CookieRun Classic Cotton Candy Cookie** is essential for hitting the leaderboard. The 2026 version of CookieRun Classic has refined the hitboxes for these letters, making it slightly easier to collect them while sliding. If you miss a Post Box, you lose out on the "Delivered Letter" bonus, which accounts for nearly 40% of her total scoring output.
