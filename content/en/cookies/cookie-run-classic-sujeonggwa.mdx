<Section icon={Swords} title="Divine Move Skill Mechanics">

The true power of Sujeonggwa Cookie lies in her unique skill, Divine Move. Unlike other cookies that rely on simple dash mechanics, Sujeonggwa Cookie introduces a strategic territorial system based on the traditional board game Go. As you run, she periodically marks territory with Go Stone Jellies. Collecting these Jellies is the key to activating her high-scoring potential.

When you collect enough Mystical Black Stone Jellies, you will create a Divine Move Black Stone Jelly. Grabbing this special jelly triggers her ultimate state. She throws an object forward—similar to Cricketer Cookie's projectile—which completely obliterates all obstacles in her path. This destruction not only clears a safe path for you but also triggers spectacular Coin fireworks and grants massive destruction points. During this state, she also temporarily gains a Mini Magnetic Aura and enters Blast mode, allowing you to sweep up every single jelly on the screen.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Best Combi Pet Synergies">

To get the absolute best performance out of Sujeonggwa Cookie, you must pair her with her designated Combi Pets. She shares her Combi Pets with Sikhye Cookie, giving players a choice between two distinct playstyles: high-score chasing or efficient coin farming.

If your goal is to set new high-score records, your go-to choice is the White Stone Teacher. This S-grade Pet provides a Combi Bonus that grants extra points for all Go Jellies and slows down your Energy drain by 6%. This slower drain is incredibly powerful in Cookie Run Classic, as even a small percentage reduction can extend your run long enough to reach an extra healing potion or a high-value bonus stage.

On the other hand, if you are looking to build up your gold reserves to upgrade other characters and treasures, the Black Stone Teacher is the perfect partner. This Pet grants a Mini Magnetic Aura and a whopping 15% Coin bonus. Combined with the Coin fireworks triggered by Sujeonggwa Cookie's obstacle destruction, this setup turns her into one of the most efficient S-grade coin farmers in the game.
