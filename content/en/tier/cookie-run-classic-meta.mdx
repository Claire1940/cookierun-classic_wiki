### Technical Troubleshooting and Requirements
A lag spike or frame drop can ruin a perfect run. Devsisters recommends specific system configurations to ensure smooth, crash-free gameplay on both iOS and Android platforms [1].

| Platform | Minimum OS Version | Recommended Free Space | Key Troubleshooting Steps |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **iOS / iPadOS** | **iOS 15.0 or later** | **2 to 3 GB** | Update iOS, close running apps, restart device, avoid jailbroken OS |
| **Android** | **AOS 8.0 or later** | **4 GB** | Update Android OS, clear app cache, free up device memory, restart |

</Callout>
